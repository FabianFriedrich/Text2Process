The MPON reports the beginning of the measuring point operations (including any measurement) at the GO.
The GO verifies the received notification of the MPON.
The GO does not accept the application of the MPON or the GO confirmes the application of the MPON.
The MPON and the MPOO perform the equipment acquisition and/or the device change.
The GO shall notify the MPON and the MPOO about the assignment of the MPON to the measuring point in terms of meter operation and any measurement.
The GO shall notify the SP and the MSPO about the confirmation of the application of the MPON.
